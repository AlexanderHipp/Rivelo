general care instructions
machine wash cool (30 degrees)
use mild detergent
close all fastenings, particularly velcro, to avoid nagging fabrics
do not use fabric softener
wash inside out, with similar colours
do not tumble dry
do not bleach
do not iron
do not dry clean
