the Rivelo philosophy

made by cyclists for cyclists

Rivelo has been designed, developed and tested by a team of cyclists in the UK, so you can ride with the confidence that every detail has been considered. trialled by casual commuters through to hard riding, long distance devotees, Rivelo garments are poised to perform in any challenge that road throws at you

clean, uncomplicated British design

inspired by the beautiful heritage behind cycling, but looking into the future. focused and well thought out, Rivelo looks the part without compromising on functionality and performance – the quiet over achieve

quality where quality counts

pushing the boundaries of innovative design & manufacturing, Rivelo gives far more than it takes. carefully selected manufacturers have been chosen for their expertise and knowledge. ergonomic seams and panelling ensure the best fitting garments whilst on the bike. industry leading chamois have been chosen to support you on even the longest rides and premium fabrics are used to ensure the best performance results.

all of this means you can just focus on enjoying your ride
